The Western Garment Fabric Studio

Where Fabric Meets Innovation

FABREEN is the dedicated fabric studio of The Apple Group, built in 2022, with a singular focus on creating high-quality, fashion-forward textiles for modern Western apparel.

Based in Surat, India’s textile heartland, FABREEN delivers end-to-end fabric solutions— from concept and design to yarn selection and finished material—through an integrated and technologically advanced manufacturing ecosystem.

Every fabric developed at FABREEN reflects precision, creativity, and a deep understanding of global fashion needs.

The Vision Behind FABREEN

Crafted by Expertise, Led by Passion

FABREEN is spearheaded by Jignesh Sheta, a textile industry veteran and founder at Apple Lifestyle Industries Limited.

With hands-on experience in dyed and printed fabric manufacturing since 2004, he brings technical depth, creative insight, and a strong design philosophy to the studio.

Under his leadership, FABREEN has grown beyond a manufacturing unit into a creative fabric laboratory—where innovation, craftsmanship, and personal commitment shape every meter of textile produced.

A Studio Built on Capabilities

From Yarn to Finished Fabric – All Under One Roof

  • Weaving, dyeing, and printing of premium fabrics
  • Expertise in viscose, nylon, polyester, and blended materials
  • Custom fabric development for Western garment applications
  • High-precision finishing and texture consistency

This integrated approach ensures seamless control over quality, timelines, and production efficiency—enabling FABREEN to deliver fabrics that are scalable and customizable.

INNOVATION AT THE CORE

FABRIC @ FABREEN

A Design Philosophy
Future-ready Authentic Bespoke Responsible Innovative Circular

At the heart of FABREEN lies its proprietary creative manifesto — FABRIC @ FABREEN. This philosophy drives a trend-led and responsible approach to textile development, combining modern design thinking with sustainable and modular production processes. Every collection is developed with a clear focus on global fashion movements, functional performance, and long-term value.

TRUSTED BY THE FASHION ECOSYSTEM

Partnering Brands, Designers & Export Markets

FABREEN supports apparel brands, fashion designers, and export-oriented manufacturers with high-performance fabrics suited for contemporary Western wear.

Its Surat-based infrastructure allows quick design-to-production cycles, making it an ideal partner for businesses that require:

  • Fast sampling and development
  • Consistent bulk quality
  • Customization across textures, prints, and finishes
  • Reliable timelines for domestic and global markets
